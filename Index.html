Choose your maximum acceptable loss — e.g. -30%. This means if the token drops 30% from your entry, the bot automatically sells your entire position.

STEP 02 👁️
Continuous Monitoring

Every open position is watched 24/7. Price is checked in real time. The bot never sleeps, never misses a dump, never freezes under pressure.

STEP 03 ⚡
Instant Auto-Exit

The moment price crosses your stop loss threshold, the bot executes an immediate sell — no delay, no waiting for confirmation, no slippage games.

STEP 04 🔒
Pro: Trailing Stop Loss

Pro mode adds a trailing stop loss — as the token pumps, the stop loss rises with it automatically. So if it dumps from the peak, you still keep most of the gain.

Standard vs Pro Stop Loss

STANDARDFixed Stop Loss

Set once at entry. If price drops X% from your buy price, bot sells. Simple, clean, effective capital protection on every trade.

PRO 😈Trailing Stop Loss

Stop loss moves up as price rises. If you're up 5x and it dumps 30%, bot exits at ~3.5x instead of losing everything. Locks in gains automatically.

PRO 😈No-Loss Mode

Once position reaches +100%, stop loss auto-moves to breakeven. You literally cannot lose money on that trade — only take profit or break even.

FAQ — Stop Loss

What stop loss % should I set?
For memecoin sniping, -30% to -50% is common. Too tight (like -10%) and you'll get stopped out on normal volatility. Too loose and you risk bigger losses on real rugs.
Does stop loss work during a rug pull?
Yes. The bot will attempt to exit immediately. In extreme rug pulls (liquidity removed), the sell may partially fill. This is why the rug filter exists — to avoid these situations entirely.
Can I turn off stop loss for specific trades?
Yes. You can override stop loss on any manual trade or disable it per position. Auto Snipe positions always use the configured stop loss for safety.
